Ayushman Bharat – PMJAY Application Data Extraction
Limited official national government sources explicitly detail all requested fields for the scheme's beneficiary enrollment or application process. The scheme is primarily entitlement-based with no formal enrollment required for eligible families listed in SECC 2011, as per official guidelines; additional verification occurs at empaneled hospitals or CSCs.
​

1. application_mode
Not specified. No official national government source clearly states online, offline, hybrid, or absence of application mode.
​

2. documents_required_count
Not specified. Official sources mention documents for verification (e.g., Aadhaar, ration card in state contexts) but no distinct national count.

3. requires_income_certificate
No. Eligibility uses SECC 2011 deprivation criteria, not income certificates.

4. requires_residence_proof
No. No mention in official national guidelines; verification uses Aadhaar or other ID via BIS at hospitals.

5. digital_only
No. Entitlement-based scheme with offline verification support via PMAMs at hospitals.

6. assisted_access_available
Yes. Pradhan Mantri Arogya Mitra (PMAM) provides assisted access for beneficiary identification and verification at empaneled hospitals.
​

7. steps_count
Not specified. Sources describe no enrollment process; hospital verification involves multiple steps like eligibility check and document upload, approximate 5-7, but not clearly defined nationally.

8. source_type
government_pdf. Primary reliance on official PDFs like AIIMS scheme manual and Odisha beneficiary guidelines.

9. source_confidence
medium. National guidelines confirm entitlement nature but lack explicit details on application fields; state docs provide process insights but vary.



PMMVY Application Data Extraction
Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) involves registration and claims submission using official forms, available online via the PMMVY portal or through Anganwadi/ASHA workers.
​
​

1. application_mode
Hybrid. Applications can be submitted online via PMMVY portal/mobile app or offline at Anganwadi centers/health facilities.
​
​

2. documents_required_count
Checklist includes Aadhaar Card of beneficiary, Identity Card of beneficiary (if no Aadhaar), Aadhaar Card of Husband, Identity Card of husband (if no Aadhaar), Aadhaar Enrolment slip of beneficiary, Aadhaar Enrolment slip of Husband, MCP Card, Page of Pass Book, distinct identity proofs listed (e.g., Voter ID, Ration Card).
​

3. requires_income_certificate
Not specified. No mention of income certificate in official forms or guidelines; eligibility based on pregnancy registration and living children count.
​
​

4. requires_residence_proof
No. Address details required in form, but no specific residence proof document mandated.
​

5. digital_only
No. Supports offline submission to Anganwadi Worker/ASHA/ANM with physical forms and documents.
​
​

6. assisted_access_available
Yes. Anganwadi Workers/ASHA workers assist in filling forms online/offline and submission.
​
​

7. steps_count
approximate 6. Form filling involves personal details entry, document attachment, undertakings, verification by field worker, supervisor/ANM check (conservative estimate from form structure).
​

8. source_type
government_pdf. Official PMMVY Form 1-A and implementation guidelines from Ministry of Women and Child Development.
​

9. source_confidence
high. Directly from official scheme form PDF on wcd.gov.in detailing exact process and requirements.


Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) Data Extraction

Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) promotes institutional deliveries through cash incentives disbursed via JSY cards completed by field workers like ASHA/ANM.
​

1. application_mode
Offline. Process uses JSY registration card filled by ANM/ASHA/AWW/link worker and submitted physically to health centers for verification.
​

2. documents_required_count
Not specified. Official guidelines reference "necessary certifications" or "BPL proofs/certificates from Panchayat/local bodies" where required (e.g., HPS states), but no distinct count or list provided nationally.
​

3. requires_income_certificate
No. No mention of income certificate; BPL certification required only in HPS states via ration card or simple criterion by gram pradhan/ward member.
​

4. requires_residence_proof
No. Referral slip from ANM determines residency for entitlement, but no specific residence proof document listed.
​

5. digital_only
No. Entire process involves physical JSY card, referral slips, and imprest cash disbursement at health institutions.
​

6. assisted_access_available
Yes. ASHA/AWW/link workers identify beneficiaries, assist with certifications, fill forms, escort to facilities, and facilitate payments.
​

7. steps_count
approximate 6. Micro-birth plan outlines steps: identification/registration, filling JSY/MCH card, collecting proofs (where necessary), submission/verification, transport arrangement, payment at institution.
​

8. source_type
government_pdf. National JSY guidelines from Ministry of Health and Family Welfare on nhm.gov.in.
​

9. source_confidence
high. Direct from central government operational guidelines detailing beneficiary process and requirements.


MJPJAY (Maharashtra) Data Extraction

MJPJAY is a cashless health insurance scheme for eligible families verified at empaneled network hospitals using ration cards or health cards, with no prior enrollment application required.

1. application_mode
Offline. Beneficiaries approach PHC/government hospitals or network hospitals for referral and verification by Arogyamitra; process handled at facilities.

2. documents_required_count
Not specified. Requires valid Yellow/Orange ration card or RGJAY/MJPJAY health card, plus photo ID (Aadhaar, driving license, election ID, etc.) for correlation; no fixed distinct count listed.

3. requires_income_certificate
No. Eligibility via yellow/orange ration card, AAY/Annapurna card, or SECC/NFSA households; no income certificate mentioned.

4. requires_residence_proof
No. No specific residence proof required; address from ration/health card used for verification.
​

5. digital_only
No. Physical documents checked by Arogyamitra at hospitals; e-preauth follows verification.
​

6. assisted_access_available
Yes. Arogyamitra at PHC, district hospitals, and network hospitals facilitate registration, verification, tests, and admission.

7. steps_count
approximate 8. Steps: approach facility for referral, Arogyamitra verification/documents check, specialist consult/tests/admission, e-preauth request/review/approval, treatment, discharge docs submission, claim settlement, follow-up.

8. source_type
official_site. Official MJPJAY scheme pages on jeevandayee.gov.in detailing beneficiary process.

9. source_confidence
high. Direct from Maharashtra government MJPJAY official site describing exact access and verification procedure.


KASP (Kerala) Data Extraction

KASP is Kerala's implementation of Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY, providing Rs. 5 lakhs health cover to eligible families based on SECC 2011 deprivation criteria without requiring prior application or enrollment.

1. application_mode
Not specified. Official sources describe beneficiary identification at hospitals using Aadhaar/ration card search in SECC database, with approval process for matches; no dedicated application mode stated.
​
​

2. documents_required_count
Not specified. Identification uses Aadhaar, ration card, or alternative family ID; no distinct list or count for any application provided.
​
​

3. requires_income_certificate
No. Eligibility per SECC deprivation/occupational criteria (D1-D5,D7, auto-included); no income certificate required.

4. requires_residence_proof
No. Residency verified via SECC database and family IDs like ration card; no separate residence proof mandated.
​

5. digital_only
No. Identification combines operator search on list, document collection (Aadhaar/ration), e-KYC, and SHA approval team review.
​
​

6. assisted_access_available
Yes. Operators/Pradhan Mantri Arogya Mitra at facilities perform search, link documents, and e-card printing; SHA team approves.
​

7. steps_count
approximate 8. Steps: operator searches list by name/location/ID, collects Aadhaar/ration/family ID, confidence score, sends for SHA approval, verifier approves/rejects, scrutiny if reject, e-card print/handover.
​

8. source_type
government_pdf. SHA-KASP service contract schedules detailing beneficiary identification on sha.kerala.gov.in.
​

9. source_confidence
high. From official Kerala SHA tender document outlining exact identification process.

​

2. documents_required_count

​

3. requires_income_certificate

​
​

4. requires_residence_proof
Not specified. No residence proof document mentioned in official guidelines or FAQ.
​

5. digital_only
No. Online application on NSP with no upload required; physical documents submitted to institute for verification.
​

6. assisted_access_available
Not specified. Verification by institute/school nodal officers, but no explicit assistance for application mentioned.
​

7. steps_count
approximate 5. Steps: register on NSP, fill online form (no upload), submit to institute, institute forwards to district, district/state verifies and sends to ministry.
​

8. source_type
government_pdf. Official guidelines and NSP FAQ from scholarships.gov.in and education.gov.in.
​
​

9. source_confidence
high. Direct from Ministry of Education guidelines and NSP FAQ listing process and documents.


Central Sector Scholarship (CSSS) Data Extraction

The Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students (CSSS), under PM-USP, is applied for online via the National Scholarship Portal (NSP) by eligible meritorious students with family income up to Rs. 4.5 lakh.
​

1. application_mode
Online. Students apply on NSP, filling details and selecting the scheme after OTR/Aadhaar login.
​

2. documents_required_count
For fresh: 12th Mark Sheet, Family Income Certificate, Category/Caste Certificate for reserved categories, Disability Certificate.
​

3. requires_income_certificate
Yes. Family Income Certificate required to verify income up to Rs. 4.5 lakh per annum.
​

4. requires_residence_proof
Not specified. No residence or domicile proof document listed in guidelines.
​

5. digital_only
No. Online application requires uploading documents and providing physical copies to institute for verification if sought.
​

6. assisted_access_available
Not specified. Institute Nodal Officers verify applications, but no explicit assistance for filling applications mentioned.
​

7. steps_count
approximate 7. Steps: OTR login/registration, select domicile/Post-Matric, fill general/category/academic details, select CSSS scheme, upload documents, submit, institute verifies.
​

8. source_type
government_pdf. Official FAQ from Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education on scholarships.gov.in.
​

9. source_confidence
high. Direct from MoE CSSS FAQ detailing application process, eligibility, and documents.
